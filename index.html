Programs

Let us make changes to the community inorder to eansure a brighter future for the children in our community

Educational Support Program

This initiative focuses on providing desks, learning materials, and scholarships to primary and secondary school students, thereby enhancing their learning environment. Recently, we successfully provided desks to 100 primary school students, significantly improving classroom conditions.

Health Awareness Campaign

Through this program, we distribute sanitary pads to secondary school girls ensuring they have access to essential hygiene products that allow them to attend school without disruption also campaigns for blood donation to save the life of mother and children

Environmental Protection Initiatives

We promote sustainability in schools by organizing tree planting days, clean-up activities, and environmental education workshops to instill a sense of responsibility towards the environment in children.

Core Values

Our core values guide our mission to support and uplift underprivileged children in our community. We are committed to making a positive impact through the following principles:

Inclusivity

We embrace diversity and ensure that every child, regardless of background, receives equal opportunities.

Empowerment

We believe in nurturing children's talents, potential, and self-confidence to become future leaders..

Community Engagement

We collaborate with local communities to create sustainable solutions for children's welfare

Innovation

We seek creative solutions to address the complex issues affecting African children.

About Us

The African Child Comforters Organisation (ACCO) originated in 2021 as a student support group, primarily focused on giving back to vulnerable communities in Mtwara Tanzania.

A group of dedicated students pooled their pocket money to assist local initiatives, which included providing sanitary pads to secondary school pupils, furnishing primary schools with desks, and promoting environmental protection programs in educational institutions.
